Sarlacc

The sarlacc (genus starwarsius) is a ground dweeling carnivore found on the desert planet of Tatooine. One of the only observed specimans was "domesticated" by a local warlord as a sacrifcal pit. While the only visible part of the sarlacc is the beak and tentecles, the cretures body reaches far below ground. Fun fact, the sarlacc takes 20,000 years to digest soemthing as small as a person.
