Gorilla

Gorillas (genus gorilla) are the largest primate on the continent of Africa. The genus of gorrila is divided into two subspecies, which are based on location. The modern gorrila shares a Family with Homo Spaians, but are not very gentically similar to Homo Sapians. The gorilla is an omnivore, consuming both plant matter and meat. They do not make great pets. In fact, it is illegal for the general public to keep a gorilla. Coming in at a very solid number four, the common gorilla is a very popular entry on the list.
